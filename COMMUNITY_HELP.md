# Community Help Wanted 🤝

We are looking for help from the community to improve **quran-cli**! If you are a developer or an enthusiast, your contributions are highly welcome.

## 📢 Notification Channels
We currently need help implementing and testing robust notification channels for:
- **Telegram Bot Integration**: Improving per-subscriber scheduling and reliability.
- **WhatsApp Support**: Exploring free/low-cost methods for prayer reminders.
- **Desktop Notifications**: Ensuring cross-platform compatibility (Windows/macOS/Linux).

## 🚧 Features Pending Completion
The following features are currently implemented but hidden from the main GUI while we polish the experience and gather feedback:
- **Browse Hadith**: Standalone Hadith browser from authentic collections.
- **Muslim World News**: Live RSS feed integration.
- **Bookmarks**: Enhanced reading position tracking.

## How to Help?
1. **GitHub Issues**: Check the issues tracker for "help wanted" tags.
2. **Pull Requests**: Submit your improvements or bug fixes.
3. **Feedback**: Share your thoughts on the design and stability of the features.

Let's build a better Islamic companion together!
